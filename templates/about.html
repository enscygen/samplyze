Samplyze

A complete, self-contained, and offline-first Laboratory Information & Management System designed for efficiency and data integrity.

Version: 1.1.2 (August 2025 Release)

Visit Enscygen

About The Application

Samplyze was built to address the need for a robust, secure, and easy-to-use management system that can operate entirely within a local network without an internet connection. It provides a comprehensive suite of tools for administrators and laboratory staff to manage the entire lifecycle of client consultations and sample analysis, from initial contact to final reporting.

The application is divided into several powerful, interconnected modules designed to streamline workflows, ensure data consistency, and enhance collaboration within the lab.

Feature Breakdown

Administrator Portal

The admin portal provides high-level control over the entire application, focusing on user management, system configuration, and data security.

  • Staff Management: Create, edit, and delete user accounts. Assign roles (Staff, Consultant) and departments.
  • Department Management: Organize your lab by creating and deleting departments.
  • System Settings: Customize the application with your lab's name, logo, and contact details.
  • Password Control: Change the admin password securely and reset staff passwords when needed.
  • Audit Trail: View a comprehensive, searchable log of all key actions performed by users across the system.
  • Backup & Restore: Create a full backup of the database and all files with a single click, or restore the entire application from a backup file.
Staff & Consultant Portal

The staff portal is the daily workspace for all lab activities, designed for efficient data entry and management.

  • Applicant Dashboard: A central hub to view and manage all applicants, with a live search filter.
  • Sample Dashboard: A comprehensive view of all samples in the lab, with live search and filtering by assigned staff.
  • Consultancy Management: Create, edit, and delete both Sampled (SC) and Non-Sampled (NSC) consultancy records.
  • Diagnosis Workflow: Add multiple, detailed diagnosis steps to any sample, complete with notes and file attachments.
  • Status Tracking: Update the status of samples as they move through the lab workflow (e.g., Submitted, In Progress, Report Ready).
Collaboration & Communication

Integrated tools to help your team work together seamlessly and share information securely within your local network.

  • Internal Mail System: A secure messaging system for staff to communicate and share file attachments, with notifications for unread mail.
  • Folder-Based File Sharing: Create shared folders, manage user-specific permissions, and upload/download files via a drag-and-drop interface.
Data & Knowledge Management

Powerful tools for standardizing data entry, ensuring consistency, and managing lab resources efficiently.

  • Equipment Logging: A complete module to track lab equipment usage, view log history, and manage your inventory.
  • Knowledge Base: A central repository for standardized diagnoses and remedies. Staff can quickly populate forms with pre-defined data, ensuring consistency and efficiency.
  • CSV Import/Export: Easily bulk-add or backup data for the Knowledge Base and Equipment Log.
  • Print-Friendly Reporting: Generate clean, professional reports for applicants and samples with a single click.
